      BEACHES
The area of Salnés is a seaside zone of the council of Pontevedra (Galicia, Spain) which is situated between the Ría de Arosa and Ría de Pontevedra.
Its coast is full of beautiful beaches with delicate and white sand, among which Isla de Arosa and Sanxenxo or Sanjenjo; el Grove, the island of La Toja and the beach La Lanzada stand out; and do not forget the beach of Vilanova de Arousa and many others.                          COUNTRY HOUSES IN THE COUNCIL OF MEIS


    The whole of the territories which are part of the council of Meis, in the area of Salnés in Pontevedra, represent a small scenery with a variety of landscapes: mountains, plains and valleys, which brings the Earth nearer to the Heaven in a beautiful mixture. Making a tour around is getting deep in the most intimate of the zone of Salnés, with the most delicious gastronomy along with vigorous and bright wines.

In the area of Salnés in Galicia, and specially its villages Sanxenxo, Portonovo, O Grove, Cambados and Vilagarcía de Arousa are amazing holiday spots to the delight of tourists due to the great range of attractive things which they offer; not only for their marvellous beaches, marinas and golf courses; but also for their cultural background and monuments. During the months of July and August it is almost impossible to find a day without any kind of festivity, accompanied with its corresponding gastronomic celebration, attached to the finest wine alvariño. Moreover there are plenty of "pazos" (Galician country palaces), historic squares, churches and monasteries which you must visit. All this implies a wide range of accommodation options; including not only hotels, but also the renting of flats or apartments, camping sites and country houses; without forgetting the renting of bedrooms at private houses.

However, if what you are looking for is to enjoy this fantastic environment but without missing the rest in a peaceful place, surrounded of vineyards, flowers and trees; with the only sound of birds waking you up in the morning, we recommend you to look for accommodation in any of the beautiful country houses of the council of Meis. From them you will be able to travel by the high speed road of Salnés, and in a few minutes you will be just in the middle of the party in Sanxenxo, in the incomparable beach of La Lanzada or in the monumental Cambados; or even by motorway, in half an hour, in Santiago de Compostela, and in a quarter time, in Pontevedra.
